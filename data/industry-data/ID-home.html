IT Skills

Skills in demand as percentile ranking, Ideal Jobs having skill as ranking 1-10. 10 being must have, 5 being basic knowledge, 1 being not required.
The skills were worked out by taking data from Burning Glass and dividing amount of jobs that have filter by the total postings it was applied to. Then was given a ranking system working with the highest percent 13% as 10 and scale down to lowest 5% at index value 6 each number represents a difference of 2% of total. The top 11 skills from Burning Glass were selected with a couple getting averaged out.
